# From Local to Global: A Graph RAG Approach to Query-Focused Summarization

Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2404.16130

GraphRAG extracts an entity graph from a corpus and builds hierarchical
community summaries. Local queries use entities and their neighborhoods; global
queries use community reports to summarize broad corpus themes. The approach is
intended for questions requiring relationships across many documents. Compared
with ordinary vector RAG, GraphRAG adds graph construction and summary cost but
can expose relation paths and global themes.
