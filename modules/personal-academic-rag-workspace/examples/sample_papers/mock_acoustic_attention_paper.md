# Attention-Guided Sound Target Detection in Noisy Scenes

Authors: Lin Demo, Qiang Scholar

Abstract: This paper proposes an attention-guided sound target detection framework for noisy acoustic scenes. It uses auditory attention cues to improve robustness under overlapping sound events.

Keywords: sound target detection, attention mechanism, noisy scenes, evaluation metrics

Year: 2025

## Introduction

Sound target detection in noisy scenes is difficult because interference masks useful acoustic patterns.

## Method

The method extracts time-frequency features, applies an attention module, and uses a classifier to identify target sound events. Attention weights emphasize salient acoustic regions.

## Experiments

The paper evaluates accuracy, F1 score, and robustness under synthetic noise. The dataset includes simulated complex acoustic scenes.

## Conclusion

Attention mechanisms help the model focus on relevant target cues and improve detection robustness.
