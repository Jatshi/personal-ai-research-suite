# Multi-Agent Paper Reading with Structured Notes

Authors: Lin Example, Qiang Scholar

Year: 2025

Keywords: paper reading, multi-agent workflow, literature review

## Abstract

This paper proposes a multi-agent workflow for generating structured paper reading notes.

## Introduction

Batch paper reading requires extracting metadata, methods, experiments, limitations, and reusable evidence.

## Method

Reader, Method, Experiment, Critic, and Writer agents exchange information through shared state.

## Experiment

The workflow is evaluated on Markdown and PDF-like paper files. Metrics include extraction completeness and report consistency.

## Discussion

The limitation is that section detection is rule-based.

## Conclusion

Shared-state multi-agent reading improves consistency of literature notes.
